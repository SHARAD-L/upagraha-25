About the Event

Embark on an exciting electronics-themed treasure hunt! Solve riddles, decode clues, and tackle challenges to uncover hidden treasures. Watt A Hunt is a thrilling journey of discovery and problem-solving. Will you outsmart the competition and claim the prize?


Round 1

Solve MCQ questions in the electronics domain within the time limit to qualify for the next round


Round 2

Navigate through three classrooms by solving questions. Each answer reveals a clue to the next room, leading to the final location where the treasure awaits.

Rules

Event Rules

Team Size: Exactly 1 peorson.
Clues cover topics like logic gates, Arduino, signal processing, and more.
Each clue advances participants to the next level.
Standard tools approved by organizers are allowed.
Smartphones or smart devices are strictly prohibited.

General

All participants must register before the event deadline
Participants should arrive 30 minutes before their scheduled session
Professional conduct is expected throughout the event
