Simul-Infinity

Description

Unleash your inner innovator at SIMUL-INFINITY! This electrifying two-round competition is designed to challenge your knowledge of sensors, programming, and electronics. Kick things off with a brain-teasing MCQ quiz, and if you make it to the top, gear up for the high-stakes Wok-Wi Design Challenge. Build, innovate, and showcase your skills under pressure—whether you go solo or team up with friends. Creativity meets competition at SIMUL-INFINITY! Step into the world of innovation and creativity!

Round 1

Test your knowledge of sensors, programming, and electronics through an exciting MCQ quiz. The top 12 teams advance!

Round 2

Take on the Wok-Wi Design Challenge! Design functional prototypes in just 45 minutes using microcontrollers, sensors, and displays. Showcase your innovation and skills while competing for the ultimate prize.

Rules

  • Team Size: Compete SOLO or in teams of up to 3 members.
  • Online Resources: Use 10 minutes for preparation with AI tools and online references (no copying premade Wok-Wi models).
  • Originality: Plagiarism, copying code, or using AI after the allocated time leads to disqualification.
  • Organizer Assistance: Assistance is limited to Wok-Wi navigation.
